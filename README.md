# movado-lbo-analysis

Movado Group (NYSE: MOV) - Private Equity Investment Analysis

Overview

This project evaluates a potential leveraged buyout of Movado Group, a publicly listed watch manufacturer and distributor.

The analysis combines:

Industry and market research
Competitive positioning analysis
Historical financial performance review
Financial forecasting
Entry valuation
Capital structure analysis
LBO return modelling
Exit strategy assessment
Investment recommendation

Key Findings
Revenue base remained relatively stable while EBITDA declined significantly.
Licensed brands represented approximately 56% of sales, creating both diversification benefits and licensing risk.
International operations generated over half of total revenue, reducing dependence on the U.S. market.
The investment generated approximately 19% IRR and 2.4x MOIC under base-case assumptions.
Final recommendation: Do not pursue the transaction due to EBITDA deterioration, limited growth prospects, and tariff exposure.
